    Lifestyle Holidays Vacation Resort on-property adventure center that has you splashing, swinging, sliding, bouncing and climbing. Choose from hockey, basketball, flag football, trampoline, miniature golf, bowling, rock wall climbing, archery, and more. The free sports and activities are endless for ages 12 and up.

    Xtreme Paintball is also offered for ages 12 and up at a member fee of $10 U.S. per game, including 40 balls and $20 for all guests, as well as Xtreme Tennis from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. daily. Games are free per hour until 7 p.m., limit of two hours per day, and $10 U.S. per hour between 7 p.m. and 10 p.m.

    Xtreme Zone is open 9 a.m. to noon, 3 p.m. to 6 p.m., and 7 p.m. to 10 p.m. daily. For activity reservations, visit the Xtreme Zone Front Desk.
