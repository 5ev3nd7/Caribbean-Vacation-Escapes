Location overview

Set on the beautiful northern coast of the Dominican Republic, the Lifestyle Holidays Vacation Resort is designed to provide the ultimate All-Inclusive vacation experience. Located at the foothills of the gorgeous mountains of Puerto Plata and right on Cofresi Beach, this resort is ideal for any family vacation, couples getaway, friend & family reunion or romantic honeymoon.

As our guest of the resort, your vacation includes VIP services and amenities including our excellent choice of restaurants and buffet dining options and unlimited beverages found at our exceptional bars. Experience the friendly hospitality of the Dominican people and serene relaxation of this tropical getaway.

At this Caribbean resort, you will have access to the warm ocean waves, sandy beach and lounging areas to soak up the sun. Everywhere you look there are beautiful landscaped grounds making this resort a paradise. Enjoy beach-party celebrations, live entertainment shows, thrilling casino nightlife and so much more. Add in a trip to Ocean World Adventure Park, Spa treatments, a round of golf, water sports, and endless island excursions and live the lifestyle you have dreamed of.


The resort is comprised of several smaller resort areas:

  • Cofresi Palm - situated along the Cofresi Beach shore
  • The Tropical - a relaxed Mediterranean-style setting
  • The Royal Suites - spacious suites surrounding a beautiful pool area
  • The Crown Suites - the comforts of home set within a tropical oasis
  • The Residence Suites - comfort and luxury for couples and families
  • The Presidential Suites - offers unique luxury accommodation
